RULES
  • The Competition is open to Diploma / Engineering Students.
  • Participants may compete as a TEAM OF MAXIMUM 4.
  • For a team of more than 4 members an Extra fees of 50 Rs per individual will have to be paid.
  • Participant must be a bonafide student of his/her college and is required to have a valid college ID card.
  • In no case an individual is allowed to participate on behalf of other participants.
  • The participant is not allowed to work in multiple teams.
  • Innovative Papers will be given credit.
  • Violation of any rule will result in permanent disqualification.
  • The decision of team AVALON shall be final and binding in case of disputes.
